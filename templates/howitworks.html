Why?


Finding jobs, especially the right jobs is a long and tedious process. You start by typing search keywords in your favorite job search sites, you get a list of jobs that contain those words. Then when you browse the listing, you find most jobs are only marginally relevant to you (or your skills), but occationally you find one or two jobs that sounds really interesting, and you tell yourself 'thats me!'.

Jobsscores saves your time by finding the right jobs for you. We use your resume, look through the job postings, and find the jobs that matches your skill sets. Each posting get a score out of 100, with score=100 means perfect match.



How the scoring is done?


The scoring takes into account what skill sets you have and what skills are desired for a particular job. It also takes into account what is the relative importnce of those skills are in general for that particular area. This means if you match 4 out of 8 required skills, that does not necessarily mean the matching score is 50, it can less or greater than 50 depending the importance of the matched skills.
